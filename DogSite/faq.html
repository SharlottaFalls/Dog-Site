Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to your dog training questions

How long does training typically take?
Training duration varies depending on your dog's needs and the chosen program. General obedience typically takes 4-6 weeks, while behavior modification might take longer. We'll provide a more specific timeline during your consultation.
What training methods do you use?
We use balanced training techniques that are effective and humane. This includes clicker training, reward-based methods, use of training tools, and strong relationship between dogs and their owners.
What age should I start training my puppy?
Puppies can start training as early as 8 weeks old. Early training is crucial for socialization and early-on prevention of behavior problems. We offer specific puppy programs designed for young dogs.
Do you offer group classes or private training?
We offer private training sessions to ensure personalized attention.
What breeds do you work with?
We work with all breeds and sizes of dogs. Our methods are adaptable to each dog's individual personality and needs, regardless of breed.
How soon can we start training?
We typically schedule new clients within a week of initial contact, depending on availability.
Do you offer virtual training sessions?
Yes, we offer both in-person and virtual training sessions to accommodate different needs.
What is your cancellation policy?
We require 24 hours notice for cancellations. Late cancellations may be subject to a fee.
Do you work with aggressive dogs?
A consultation is required first. Some owners might mistake aggression for reactivity which are two completely different issues, so a thorough assessment over phone or video call is needed first.
